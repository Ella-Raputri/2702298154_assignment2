Our Final Project

NMC

Our client is BNMC (Binus Mandarin Club). We decided to make a website for competition management and helpdesk ticketing system for them to ease their process in managing their annual major program, which is the National Mandarin Competition (NMC). So, there are 2 main features in an app.

Customer can register to a competition and send tickets to ask the committee about the competition. Meanwhile, the admin can see the participants of a competition and answer the customer's questions.
