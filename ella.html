SchedNara

SchedNara is an abbreviation of Schedule of Nara. Nara is what the Aranaras in Genshin call humans. So, SchedNara is the schedule of humans, or to be exact, the schedule of the user. SchedNara is used to set the tasks and workflows of the user. Besides that, it also allows the user to interact with some Aranaras and ask them some trivial things.
